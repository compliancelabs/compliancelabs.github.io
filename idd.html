Initial Disclosure Document

Regulatory Transparency Framework

01. Whose products do we offer?

Compliance Labs & Capital Ltd offers a specialized range of commercial finance products including Asset Finance, Invoice Finance, and Revolving Credit Facilities. We act as an intermediary and work with a restricted panel of institutional lenders who specialize in the construction sector. We do not offer products from the entire market.

02. Which service will we provide you with?

We will advise and make a recommendation for you after we have assessed your needs through our **Operational Intelligence** protocols. We act as a credit broker, not a lender. Our role is to pre-vet your financial data to ensure application success and introduce you to suitable lenders.

03. What will you have to pay us for our services?

For our **Compliance Labs** services, we charge a fixed monthly recurring fee as outlined in your engagement letter. For **Capital Placement** (Brokerage), we do not typically charge you a direct fee; however, we will receive a commission or payment from the lender should you decide to enter into an agreement with them. The amount of commission varies by lender and product.

04. Confidentiality & Complaints

Your privacy is paramount. All data shared for the purpose of a finance application is held in an encrypted institutional data room. If you have a complaint regarding our brokerage service, please contact the Managing Director directly at office@compliancelabs.capital.
