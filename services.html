Our core mandate. We deploy the Payment Firewall™ to verify CIS status in real-time, preventing the £10k 'Deemed Labour' liability trap. We enforce the Construction Act 1996 to recover retentions.

Logistics & Haulage

Managing fleet transition risks. We provide the capital bridge for Euro-6 upgrades and audit driver status to prevent IR35 reclassification liabilities.

Heavy Industry & Waste

High-depreciation asset management. We structure finance for crushers and screens while managing Landfill Tax credit audits.
