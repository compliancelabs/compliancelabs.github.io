Complaints Procedure.

01. Our Commitment

Compliance Labs & Capital Ltd is committed to providing institutional-grade service. If our protocols or brokerage services fail to meet your expectations, we provide a transparent, time-bound resolution process.

02. The Formal Process

Phase 1: Acknowledgement
Upon receipt of a written complaint to compliance@compliancelabs.capital, we will acknowledge your concern within 5 working days.

Phase 2: Investigation
Our compliance officer will review the case data. We aim to provide a final response within 4 weeks. In complex cases involving external lenders, this may extend to 8 weeks as per FOS standards.

03. Escalation

If you are dissatisfied with our final response regarding a credit brokerage matter, you may have the right to refer your complaint to the Financial Ombudsman Service (FOS).
