Questions we get asked

Do you work with sole-traders or only limited companies?

Both. We simply need a valid UTR or Company House number to verify CIS status.

What if the debt is older than 90 days?

We still chase, but success probability drops. We’ll advise upfront if we think legal action is smarter.

Are you FCA-regulated?

No—our service is administrative. We do not offer credit or insurance products.

Can we upgrade or downgrade mid-contract?

Yes. Change tier any time from your dashboard; adjustments are pro-rated to the day.

Is there a setup fee?

Zero. We even import your existing subbie list during onboarding at no cost.
