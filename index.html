What is a "Payment Firewall"?

It is our proprietary integration that links your payment processing to live HMRC verification data. If a sub-contractor's status is invalid or unverified, the firewall prevents the payment transaction, eliminating human error fines at the absolute point of origin.

How does the "7-Step Recovery" differ from debt chasing?

Standard debt chasing is based on persistence. Our 7-Step Recovery is based on legal leverage. We deploy the Construction Act 1996 and Statutory Interest demands to create immediate financial pressure on the debtor, moving your invoice to the top of their priority list.

Are you regulated for Commercial Finance?

We operate as a specialist commercial finance intermediary, partnering with FCA-authorized institutional lenders. We focus specifically on Commercial Asset and Invoice finance, using your verified 'Labs' data to de-risk applications and secure preferential rates.
