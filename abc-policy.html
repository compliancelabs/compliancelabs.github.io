Charter: Professional Ethics

Anti-Bribery & Corruption.

01. Policy Overview

It is the policy of Compliance Labs & Capital Ltd to conduct business in an honest and ethical manner. We take a zero-tolerance approach to bribery and corruption and are committed to acting professionally, fairly, and with integrity in all our business dealings.

02. Prohibited Conduct

No employee or representative of The Firm shall offer, give, solicit, or accept any bribe, kickback, or improper financial incentive. This includes payments intended to influence the selection of a credit provider or the outcome of a compliance audit.
