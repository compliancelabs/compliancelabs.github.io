Charter: Supply Chain Ethics

Modern Slavery Statement.

01. Organizational Stance

Compliance Labs & Capital Ltd holds a zero-tolerance position toward modern slavery and human trafficking. We recognize the construction sector as a high-risk industry and take proactive steps to ensure our operations and supply chains remain ethical.

02. Due Diligence Protocols

Our Payment Firewall™ protocol includes checks on sub-contractor business structures. We verify that all partners adhere to the Modern Slavery Act 2015 and provide fair working conditions. We monitor for indicators of exploitation, such as multiple workers sharing a single bank account or address.

03. Accountability

This statement is made pursuant to section 54(1) of the Modern Slavery Act 2015 and constitutes our firm’s slavery and human trafficking statement for the current financial year.
